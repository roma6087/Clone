About Us

One skill can transform a life—at Anudip, we’ve empowered 500,000 and are committed to reaching millions more. Since 2007, we have been equipping youth and women in crisis with in-demand skills in coding, AI, and professional development, helping them break free from poverty. Our partnerships with Accenture, Microsoft, Wells Fargo, J.P. Morgan, and the Michael & Susan Dell Foundation ensure industry-aligned training that leads to meaningful careers.

With 70% of our graduates securing life-changing jobs and 53% being female, we’ve seen families rise from generations of struggle. Through 90+ learning centers across India, we uplift marginalized communities, refugees, and people with disabilities. Our students go on to work with Accenture, iMerit, Capgemini, Tech Mahindra, Axis Bank, Infosys, and more—proving that when ambition meets opportunity, transformation is inevitable.

Event Highlights

Anudip Foundation hosted “Reskilling for Tomorrow: AI & Workforce 2.0”, a dynamic event spotlighting the transformative power of AI in reshaping the future of work and empowering marginalized youth which was graced by Chief guest of honour – Dr. Sukanta Majumdar, Minister of State for Education and DoNER, Govt of India. The gathering brought together thought leaders from industry, academia, and civil society to delve into AI’s impact on the job market and chart a collaborative path to bridge skill gaps within underserved communities. From expert panels and inspiring success stories to certificate distributions and future-forward discussions, the event celebrated innovation, inclusivity, and the power of digital skills.

Bridging Dreams to Reality

Since 2007, Anudip Foundation has empowered over 500,000 youth and 200,000 women, equipping them with essential digital and soft skills for a brighter future. With 90+ Skills and Career Development Centers, a dedicated team of 900+ members, and partnerships with 400+ corporates, Anudip ensures a 70% job placement rate and a 300% increase in family income. Operating in 22 Indian states and 3 countries, Anudip transforms lives by turning aspirations into achievements, proving that when opportunity meets determination, success is within reach.
